IMPULSE OR AGAINST IMPULSE

——working under question1

An daily use pen designed for balancing between the environment and impulses.

INTRODUCTION

 Our project is concentrated on the concept of “impulse”. We choose the pressing pen as our main project, because most people have an “impulse” to press the pen for its on-off function and the sensory experience it brings .
Users may think they have the power to choose what object they want and how they use it, but actually they don't. The user's choice is based on a certain impulse, which is likely to be predicted and designed by the designer. It is, therefore, an illusion of choice.
 

AIMS

We will study the "impulse" experimentally choosing the pressing pen as the main object, studying the design factors that trigger certain impulses (change the factor to increase or decrease the impulse). So, the process and results of our experiment will be presented, and the final design product will be derived on this basis.
Briefly, our aim is to study the relationship between designs and the impulse they generate in order to answer the question “Who holds the power”, and help people balance between the environment and their impulses. 

OBJECTIVES

According to the results of the experimental study, we conclude the pen that meets the experimental objectives. These pressing pens will satisfy: 1) relieving anxiety and promoting people to adapt to the environment; 2) creating illusions to help people control their impulses.

DESIGN DEVELOPMENT

An everyday use pressing pen is developed. It is improved according to the results of the experiment, in order to help people balance between the environment and their impulses.

INTERACTIVE PROFILING II (30-50 years old)

USER FLOW


This is a daily commonly used pen, its main function is to write records, the handwriting is black, the thickness is 0.5mm. It is mainly used in office, classroom, outdoor and so on, with a wide range of applications; it is suitable for people aged from 6 to 50 years old. It is mainly used by office crowd and education group.


When you need to relieve tension and anxiety, pressing the pen is very attractive to you.The feature of this product is that it has been improved to help people balance their environment and their own impulses. Specific use scenarios: when we are in a specific application site, we need to keep the environment quiet, at the same time, we need to keep our mental state focused and avoid excessive pressure and tension. 

You can take up the pen and press it. The soft and comfortable feeling of the pressing part can release your emotion. At the same time, it will not make a sound, so it will not let the people around you notice your emotion. In addition, you can also find that it can't be pressed down directly. Such reaction forces make you find your emotions highly tense, and begin to make positive self-regulation, so as to adapt to the environment and return to calm, focused work and study.

EXPERIMENT

——"Press?Which?"

The video shows the user test.

Experiment-result



1. Among the four senses, vision has the greatest impact on user behavior. Visual shocks are the most impulsive.

2. The smell of the pressing place has the least influence on the user's impulsion. 

The most impulse design factors

1. SIGHT: Make the pressing part exactly fits the shape of user's finger.
2. TOUCH: Soft silica gel.
3. SMELL: With fragrance.
4. SOUND: The quieter and more muffled sound. 

The least impulse design factors

1. SIGHT: Use the sponge to make the pressing part.
2. TOUCH: Rough paper.
3. SMELL: /
4. SOUND: The louder and higher sound. 

SKETCH

Size：As the sketch shows
Material：Plastic for the main part and the soft silica gel for the pressing part.
Colour：Ash white
Character：The pressing part is a little elastic and neither too hard nor too soft.
Reason：Based on the result of our experiment. 

FINAL DESIGN

We conclude all these findings and design a deceptive pen. Its design features:
1.It creates the “illusion of impulse” when the user tries to press it. The press part is attractive, allowing the user to release the impulse in the action of pressing and relieve the anxiety.
2. It can‘t really press so that users can restrain their own impulses in time. Also, it doesn't bring noise to the environment.
